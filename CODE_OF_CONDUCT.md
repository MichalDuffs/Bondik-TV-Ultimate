# 🤝 Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The Bondik TV Ultimate community is built on respect, cooperation and sharing knowledge.

We welcome everyone who wants to help improve the project.

---

## Expected Behavior

Please:

- Be respectful.
- Help others.
- Share constructive feedback.
- Respect different opinions.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Not allowed:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Spam
- Malicious contributions

---

## Responsibility

Project maintainers may remove content or contributions that violate these principles.

---

Thank you for helping create a friendly community.
